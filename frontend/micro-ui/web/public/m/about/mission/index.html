Our Mission

Vision

"A future in which courts go to people, instead of people going to courts. A future where courts proactively deliver and enable just-in-time services to people shaped around their unique needs, preferences, circumstances, and location."

The 24x7 Open and Networked (ON) Courts are starting with cheque dishonour cases under Section 138 of the NI Act, which comprise over 10% of criminal pendency in India. This initiative aligns with Phase III of India's E-Courts Project.

Guiding Principles

People Centric

Prioritising user needs for a seamless, predictable, and efficient experience at every step of the judicial process.

Open

Actionable information, open technology, and accessible data — subject to privacy — for all stakeholders.

Networked Ecosystem

Connecting judicial systems, people, and legal representation to enable efficient and transparent dispute resolution.

ON Courts Experience

Accessible & Predictable

Access case files, summons, and notices from any device, anytime, without visiting the court.

Assisted & Empowering

Real-time updates on payments, hearings, and orders. Alerts for required actions with easy-to-use templates and dashboards.

Seamless & Frictionless

Eliminates manual tasks like signing physical documents, travelling for payments, or tracking notices manually.
